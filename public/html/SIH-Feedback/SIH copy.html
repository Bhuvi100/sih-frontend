National Technology Week 2023

Held from 11th May to 14th May, 2023 at Pragati Maidan, New Delhi.

The Govt. of India celebrated National Technology Week 2023 with involvement of 11 different ministries and Government departments, including Office of Principal Scientific Advisor, Ministry of Education's Innovation Cell/AICTE where different startups exhibited. During this event, a dedicated pavilion on a theme of Making India Global Innovation Hub was set up by the AICTE/Ministry of Education's Innovation Cell. This event provided a good opportunity for the MIC/AICTE supported innovation team to showcase their innovations and efforts, and connect with the ecosystem enablers. The Event was a Grand Success and it showcased a Brilliant Startup Ecosystem of our Nation.

Name: KAUSTAV MANDAL
Designation: Founder’s Office - Product & Growth
Organization: GrabOn & WriteGeniusAI
Contributed as: Design Mentor
In which SIH you were participated in : SIH 2020

Any overall feedback for the event?

There is a lot of scope for improvement that I felt as a product person. Firstly, some ministries just came up with a problem statement just for the sake of putting forward the hackathon and didn’t back it up with enough data to approach the problem, ultimately contributing to weak solutions where many students were clueless about what to do next. Secondly, there is no official code repository for keeping the developments recorded, resulting in a waste of time, effort, and revenue for the problem-setting bodies. Thirdly, most importantly, the winning teams should be captured immediately by the POCs of Ministries to motivate them to go further beyond just the MVP and build real-world solutions out of it with proper incubation, which I felt was missing, and willing mentors should also be appointed as consultants who are willing to spend their valuable time in building the next unicorn maybe!
