Patil Mitesh

Designation: Package Consultant

Organization: IBM

Contributor: Design Mentor

In which SIH you were participated in :

  • SIH 2019

Overall feedback for the event?

"As a design mentor for SIH 2023, I must commend the participants for their dedication, creativity, and perseverance throughout the competition. It was truly inspiring to witness the innovative solutions and designs that emerged from the teams. However, there are areas where improvement can be made. Firstly, while the teams demonstrated impressive technical skills and problem-solving abilities, there were instances where the product readiness and quality fell short. It is essential for teams to prioritize thorough testing and refinement to ensure that their solutions are robust and effective. Secondly, the pitch preparation for the final presentations could have been more polished and compelling. Effective communication is key in conveying the value proposition of a product, and teams should focus on refining their messaging and presentation skills to effectively capture the attention of the judges and stakeholders. Lastly, there were significant hardware challenges encountered by several teams, particularly in areas such as circuit design, interfacing, and integration. It is crucial for mentors to provide timely guidance and support to help teams overcome these obstacles and ensure the successful implementation of their designs. Overall, SIH 2023 was a valuable learning experience for both mentors and participants alike. I believe that with continued dedication, collaboration, and refinement, the teams have the potential to further enhance their designs and make a meaningful impact in their respective fields."
